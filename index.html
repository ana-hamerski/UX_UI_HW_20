Overview

Addressing the gap in current market offerings, Roaming Xplore app is designed to meet the needs of travellers who use social media for trip planning but struggle to find authentic local experiences. Focusing on personalization and trust, the app connects users with local guides and provides curated, off-the-beaten-path itineraries. This approach aims to resolve the pain points of our target users and enhance their overall travel experience.

My Role

As the sole product designer, my role spanned the entire app development, from initial user research and conceptual design to prototyping. I focused on a thorough design process to tailor every aspect of the app to our users' specific needs and preferences.

Background & Motivation

Social media greatly influences travel planning, with 52% of travelers choosing destinations based on such platforms (Science Direct). This trend is especially strong among younger travelers, with 81% under 35 and 53% of Gen Z using social media for travel inspiration (Phocuswright, HospitalityNet). Recognizing a gap in the market for authentic local experiences, our project aims to create a platform that connects travelers with local guides. This aligns with the preferences of 35% of travelers using social media for trip ideas (EWP Marketing), and caters to the significant portion influenced by social media in making travel decisions, like dining (Passport Photo Online).

User Insights

During user interviews, we discovered that younger travelers (under 30) struggle to find authentic local experiences while relying on social media platforms for trip planning and inspiration. They also miss inclusive options available for people with disabilities, mainly the physical ones.

Problem Statement

How might we integrate insights from social media platforms to offer travelers unique, personalized local experiences (including accessible options), connecting them with local guides for authentic, off-the-beaten-path adventures?

How am I going to address the problem?

Feature Prioritization

  1. Tools to connect travelers with locals and personalize their suggested places to visit

    I want to create a community through the app using social media UI patterns to allow users to connect with locals and motivate users to follow, share, chat, and save resources.

  2. Accessible travel options for users with disabilities

    I want to add a filter option and an icon near the place's name to allow users to recognize when inclusive options are available.

  3. Offer a virtual tour guide feature

    By adding this feature, users will be able to easily review the place before starting the booking process through a video and not only photos.

Value Proposition Statement

Roaming Xplore: Unlock Authentic Adventures and Connect with Your Travel Tribe.
